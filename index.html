About

Welcome to Bates Cat Rescue, a sanctuary for our feline friends in need. Founded in 2024, our mission is simple: to provide a safe and loving environment for cats who have been abandoned, abused, or neglected.

At Bates’, we believe that every cat deserves a chance at a happy and fulfilling life. Our dedicated team of volunteers works tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome cats of all ages, breeds, and backgrounds. From tiny kittens to senior cats, we are committed to giving each cat the care, attention, and support they need to thrive.

Our rescue center provides a temporary haven for cats while they await their forever homes. We ensure that they receive proper medical care, nutritious meals, and plenty of socialization and enrichment activities. All cats are spayed/neutered before adoption to help control the pet population and prevent future homelessness.

But our work doesn't stop once a cat finds a new family. We offer ongoing support and resources to adopters to ensure a smooth transition and promote responsible pet ownership. We also advocate for animal welfare and raise awareness about the importance of adoption, spaying/neutering, and compassionate care for all animals.

Whether you're looking to adopt a new feline friend or simply want to support our cause, we invite you to join us in making a difference in the lives of cats in need. Together, we can create a world where every cat is valued, cherished, and loved. Welcome to Bates Cat Rescue – where every cat's story deserves a happy ending.
